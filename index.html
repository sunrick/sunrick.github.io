about

personal

who am i

i'm half swedish half american and i've been really fortunate to grow up across the world. i like to joke around, kick a soccer ball with buddies and ride motorcycles.

why i get up in the morning

i like learning something new everyday. i love learning languages, learning about cultures, and about technology. i'm very passionate about combining technology with education. also my friends and family keep me going.

fun facts

i was born on friday the 13th. i can make grilled cheese sandwiches. arsenal is my favorite football team. i really like teaching and coaching little kids.

programming

past

i started "programming" when i was 10 by building multiplayer scenario maps in age of kings, spawning units out of the ground, setting conditions for victory and other fun stuff. i also remember building websites in microsoft frontpage (lol).

present

until recently programming as a career felt out of reach to me. i wrote python scripts for fun, and used vba for excel for work. one day i decided i had enough and i enrolled at the iron yard. now i'm happy to say i can develop full-stack web applications with ruby on rails!

future

i'm really excited about everything there is to learn! despite having spent most of my time working on the back-end, i have a soft spot for making websites pretty, cool and fun to use. i'm really enjoying learning about javascript, jquery, css frameworks and angular.
